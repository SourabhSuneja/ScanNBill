Privacy Policy

ScanNBill – Smart Mini PoS for Retailers

1. Introduction

ScanNBill is designed to help small retailers and shopkeepers manage their business efficiently by turning a smartphone into a mini Point of Sale (PoS) system.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. We ensure that your data stays completely under your control.

2. Data Collection

ScanNBill does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Product Information: Name, brand, price, quantity (entered by user)
  • Sales Data: Transactions recorded by the user
  • Shop Information: Shop name, owner name, etc.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on your device using local storage.

No data is uploaded, shared, or synced with any server or third party.
If you uninstall the app or reset your device, all stored data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera: Required for scanning product barcodes
  • Local Storage: Required to store app data on your device

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and nothing else.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you have full control over your information. We do not have access to your data at any point.

6. Third-Party Services

ScanNBill does not use any third-party services that collect or process user data.
